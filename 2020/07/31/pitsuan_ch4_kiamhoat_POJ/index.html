Pit-sòan ê Chho͘-ha̍k: Kiám-hoat

Kiám-hoat

Lâi pí-phēng nn̄g tiâu-siàu, ēng khah-sòe-tiâu khàu-tû khah tōa-tiâu khòaⁿ ū chhun jōa-chōe, hō chòe Kiám-hoat. Kiám-hoat Ka hoat saⁿ-tùi-hoán.

Khah-tōa-tiâu-siàu tio̍h thán-hoâiⁿ siá chòe téng-lia̍t, khah-sòe-tiâu tio̍h hoâiⁿ-lia̍t ē-bīn; toaⁿ ê ūi tùi toaⁿ ê ūi, cha̍p ê ūi tùi cha̍p ê ūi, í-siōng kok tùi i ê ūi pâi. Pâi-lia̍t bêng-pe̍k, tio̍h u̍ih-hûn keh-kài; tāi-seng tùi toaⁿ ê uī kiám-khí, ēng ē-siàu kiám téng-siàu, khòaⁿ chhun jōa-chōe, chiū kì tī pún-ūi ê hûn-ē. Kok-ūi tio̍h chiàu chit-ê hoat-tō͘.

\[ \begin{array}{r} 3879\\ 1326\\ \hline 2571 \end{array} \]

Beh tùi \(3897\) lâi kiám \(1326\), khoàⁿ kai chhun jōa-chōe; tio̍h chiong tōa siàu siá téng-bīn lia̍t, sió siàu siá ē-tóe lia̍t, u̍ih-hûn keh-kài; khòaⁿ toaⁿ-ūi ê \(7\) kiám \(6\) sīn \(1\), tio̍h kì \(1\) tī pún-ūi hûn-ē; cha̍p ūi ê \(9\) kiám \(2\) sīn \(7\), pah-ūi ê \(8\) kiám \(3\) sīn \(5\), chheng-ūi ê \(3\) kiám \(1\) sīn \(2\), kok tùi pún-ūi ê hûn-ē lâi kì, chiū chai tōa siàu só͘ chhun chiū-sī \(2571\).

Ha̍k-seng teh-sǹg ê sî, tio̍h ho͘ kóng, \(7\) kiám \(6\), sīn \(1\), kì \(1\). \(9\) kiám \(2\), sīn \(7\), kì \(7\). \(8\) kiám \(3\), sīn \(5\), kì \(5\). \(3\) kiám \(1\), sīn \(2\), kì \(2\).

Ē-bīn chiah ê siàu tio̍h sûi-tiâu kiám khòaⁿ chhun jōa-chōe.

  1. \(5\) kiám \(3\); \(6\) kiám \(4\); \(7\) kiám \(4\); \(8\) kiám \(3\); \(9\) kiám \(5\); \(11\) kiám \(8\); \(12\) kiám \(8\).
  2. \(23\) kiám \(11\); \(34\) kiám \(12\); \(57\) kiám \(24\); \(68\) kiám \(34\); \(96\) kiám \(51\); \(95\) kiám \(91\); \(86\) kiám \(85\).
  3. Tùi \(364\) kiám \(222\); tùi \(607\) kiám \(401\); tùi \(670\) kiám \(560\); tùi \(43105\) kiám \(21003\).
  4. Só͘ pâi-lia̍t ê siàu tio̍h kiám: \[ \begin{array}{r} 7649\\4227\\\hline\phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 89546\\73210\\\hline\phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 97600\\35200\\\hline\phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 86976454\\75716254\\\hline\phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 64375\\33333\\\hline\phantom{0} \end{array} \]

Kiám-chhái ē-lia̍t ê ūi ū siàu khah tōa téng-lia̍t saⁿ-tùi ê ūi, bô kàu-gia̍h thang-kiám, chiū tio̍h tùi chêng-ūi chioh-chi̍t hòa cha̍p lâi thiⁿ pún-ūi téng-lia̍t ê siàu, jiân-āu lâi kiám; só͘-chhun-ê kì lok pún-ūi ê hûn-ē, tùi chêng-ūi só͘ chioh ê chi̍t tio̍h hêng lâi thiⁿ chêng-ūi ê ē-lia̍t.

\[ \begin{array}{r} 18629\\ 9573\\ \hline 9056 \end{array} \]

Tùi \(18629\) kiám \(9573\); khòaⁿ kai chhun jōa-chōe; tio̍h chiàu hoat-tō͘ pâi-lia̍t, u̍ih-hûn keh-kài; khòaⁿ toaⁿ-ūi ê \(9\) kiám \(3\), sīn \(6\), tio̍h kì \(6\) tī pún-ūi ê hûn-ē, cha̍p ūi ê \(2\) kiám \(7\), bô kàu kiám, tio̍h tùi chêng-ūi chioh chi̍t hòa cha̍p, thiⁿ chham chòe \(12\), chiū sīn \(5\), lâi kì \(5\) lok hûn-ē; só͘ chioh ê chi̍t tio̍h thiⁿ hêng chêng-ūi ê \(5\) chòe \(6\); taⁿ \(6\) kiám \(6\) sī tùi-tú, tio̍h kì \(0\) lok hûn-ē; \(8\) kiám \(9\), bô kàu kiám tio̍h tùi chêng-ūi chioh chi̍t hòa cha̍p thiⁿ-chham chòe \(18\), chiū-sīn \(9\), lâi-kì \(9\) lok hûn-ē; só͘ chioh ê chi̍t tio̍h kì \(0\) lok hûn-ē, nā-sī chîn-tiâu ê ūi kì \(0\) bô lō͘-ēng, chiah m̄-sái kì: chiū chai só͘ chhun chiū-sī \(9056\).

Ha̍k-seng teh-sǹg ê sî, tio̍h ho͘ kóng, \(9\) kiám \(3\), sīn \(6\), kì \(6\). \(2\) kiám \(7\), bô kàu kiám, chioh \(10\), \(12\) kiám \(7\), sīn \(5\), kì \(5\). \(5\) hoân \(1\) chòe \(6\), \(6\) kiám \(6\) tùi-tú, kì \(0\). \(8\) kiám \(9\), bô kàu kiám, chioh \(10\), \(18\) kiám \(9\), sīn \(9\), kì \(9\). Hoân \(1\), \(1\) kiám \(1\), tùi-tú, m̄-sái kì.

Chit-ê chioh koh hêng ê hong hoat, kiám-chhái ū lâng bōe bêng-pe̍k, chiū tio̍h siūⁿ-tùi chêng-ūi chioh chi̍t chiū hit-ūi tio̍h khah-chió, nā-sī bô hō͘ i khah-chió, sòa thiⁿ ē-lia̍t chiū-sī saⁿ-tâng.

\[ \begin{array}{r} 4345\\2826\\\hline1519 \end{array} \]

Khòaⁿ piⁿ-tiâu só͘ pâi-lia̍t ê siàu. \(5\) kiám \(6\) bô thang kiám, tùi chêng-ūi ê \(4\) (chiū-sī \(4\) cha̍p) chioh \(1\) cha̍p lâi thiⁿ-chham chòe \(15\) kiám \(6\) chhun \(9\). Taⁿ tùi cha̍p ūi ê \(4\) ū chioh \(1\) tio̍h chhun \(3\), \(3\) kiám \(2\) chhun \(1\). Nā-sī khòaⁿ téng-lia̍t iû-goân chòe \(4\), lâi thiⁿ ē-lia̍t ê cha̍p ūi chòe \(3\); \(4\) kiám \(3\) chhun \(1\), iā-sī án-ni. Chit-ê hoat tō͘, khah hó in-ūi kiám téng-lia̍t khah ōe chhò-gō͘. Só͘-í siat chit-ê chioh-hêng ê hoat-tō͘.

\[ \begin{array}{r} 4\\2\\\hline 2 \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 14\\12\\\hline 2 \end{array} \]

Koh khòaⁿ piⁿ-tiâu só͘ pâi-lia̍t ê siàu chiū kiám-chhái ōe khah bêng.

4 kiám \(2\) chhun \(2\). Téng-lia̍t ē-lia̍t pîⁿ-pîⁿ tio̍h thiⁿ \(10\), chòe \(14\), \(12\), iû-goân chhun \(2\).

Taⁿ tio̍h koh khòaⁿ pún-siàu.

\[ \begin{array}{r} 4345\\2826\\\hline 1519 \end{array} \]

5 kiám \(6\) bô thang kiám, chiū téng-lia̍t tio̍h thiⁿ \(10\), chòe \(15\). Lóng m̄-sái kóng chioh, iā m̄-sái gī-lūn hit-ê \(10\) tùi tah-lo̍h lâi, kan-ta thiⁿ-chham \(10\) tiāⁿ-tiāⁿ. Kiám \(6\) chhun \(9\). Taⁿ in-ūi téng-lia̍t í-keng ū thiⁿ-chham \(10\), chiū ē-lia̍t iā tio̍h thiⁿ \(10\), chóng-sī cha̍p-ūi lâi thiⁿ, tio̍h thiⁿ \(1\) tiāⁿ-tiāⁿ (chiū-sī \(1\) cha̍p), \(2\) thiⁿ \(1\) kiōng \(3\). \(4\) kiám \(3\) chhun \(1\). \(3\) (chiū-sī \(3\) pah) kiám \(8\) (chiū-sī \(8\) pah) bô thang kiám \(8\) chhun \(5\) (chiū-sī \(5\) pah). Ē-lia̍t thiⁿ \(10\) (10 pah chiū-sī \(1\) chheng). \(2\) thiⁿ \(1\) kiōng \(3\). \(4\) kiám \(3\) chhun \(1\).

Chiah ê siàu tio̍h pâi-lia̍t lâi sǹg.

  1. Tùi \(42134\) lâi kiám \(18765\); tùi \(46622\) lâi kiám \(38742\); tùi \(40000\) lâi kiám \(20040\).
  2. Góa khiàm lâng \(1860\) chîⁿ, chiū hō͘-i chi̍t kho͘-gûn ōe ōaⁿ-tit \(1190\) chîⁿ: tio̍h koh hō͘-i chîⁿ jōa-chōe?
  3. Lâng ū chîⁿ \(11345\) chiū the̍h-chhut \(9203\): iáu chhun jōa-chōe-mah?
  4. Só͘ pâi-lia̍t ê siàu tio̍h khàu-tû khòaⁿ si̍t jōa-chōe: \[ \begin{array}{r} 135876\\ 96789\\ \hline \phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 10000\\ 9999\\ \hline \phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 101010\\ 89899\\ \hline \phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 101001000\\ 99999999\\ \hline \phantom{0} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{r} 101\\ 11\\ \hline \phantom{0} \end{array} \]
  5. Tùi chi̍t-bān lâi khàu-tû cha̍p-ê; tùi cha̍p-ek lâi khàu-tû chi̍t-ê; tùi cha̍p-ek liân cha̍p bān lâi khàu-tû káu ek káu chheng liân káu-cha̍p-káu.
  6. Tùi chi̍t-pah-bān liân cha̍p-it khàu-tû káu-pah liân káu-ê; Tùi jī-cha̍p-gō͘-bān khàu-tû jī-cha̍p-sì-bān liân cha̍p-ê; Tùi cha̍p tiāu khàu-tû cha̍p-ek.
  7. Hâm-hong goân-nî chiū-sī Kiù-chú kàng-seng \(1851\) nî, Tông-tī goân-nî chiū-sī \(1862\). Mn̄g Hâm-hong chē-ūi kúi-nî?
  8. Góa khì bó͘ lâng ê tiàm-thâu bóe pò͘, ta̍t chîⁿ \(867\), chiū hō͘-i chi̍t-kho͘-gûn thang ōaⁿ \(1113\) chîⁿ, i tio̍h chāu góa jōa-chōe chîⁿ?
  9. Tāi-chheng thâu-chi̍t-ê hông-tè, Sūn-tī goân-nî chiū-sī Kiù-chú kàng-seng \(1644\) nî; tùi hit-sî kàu-taⁿ kúi-nî?
  10. Ēng \(123\), \(456\), \(789\) lâi siong-ka, tùi só͘ tit-tio̍h ê chóng-siàu khàu-tû \(658\): iáu chhun jōa-chōe?
  11. Bó͘-lâng chi̍t-ji̍t ū thàn \(125\) chîⁿ, koh chi̍t-ji̍t ū thàn \(150\), iū koh chi̍t-ji̍t ū thàn \(186\), chiū ēng \(225\) chîⁿ lâi tia̍h bí: Iáu chhun jōa-chōe? Tek \(235\) chîⁿ.
  12. Tùi \(1100010\) tio̍h khàu-tû \(123456\), \(654321\) ê chóng-siàu, khòaⁿ chhun jōa-chōe. Tek \(322233\).
  13. Cha̍p-poeh séng ê lâng-gia̍h chiū-sī téng-bīn Ka-hoat hit-phiⁿ só͘ kì-chài: Taⁿ-mn̄g, Hok-kiàn chió Sù-chhoan jōa-chōe lâng? Kńg-sai chió Hok-kiàn jōa-chōe? Hûn-lâm Kùi-chiu ha̍p-kiōng chió Kńg-tang jōa-chōe? Chiat-kang chió Tit-lē jōa-chōe? Ô͘-lâm Kńg-sai ha̍p-kiōng chió Ô͘-pak jōa-chōe? Kang-so͘ chió Ô͘-lâm chham Ô͘-pak jōa-chōe?
  14. Bó͘ lâng chòe seng-lí, thâu-chi̍t nî ū thàn \(300\) niú, tē-jī nî ū thàn \(485\) niú, tē-saⁿ nî ū sih7 \(277\) niú, tē-sì nî ū thàn \(1230\) niú, tē-gō͘ nî ū si̍h \(820\) niú, tē-la̍k nî ū thàn \(3566\) niú: Taⁿ mn̄g, Chí la̍k-nî-lāi siōng thàn jōa-chōe? Tek \(4844\) niú.
  15. Nn̄g-lâng Choân-chiu khí-sin kiâⁿ siâng-lō͘, chi̍t-lâng khiâ-bé, chi̍t-lâng pō͘-kiâⁿ; khiâ-bé-ê ta̍k-ji̍t kiâⁿ \(95\) lí, pō͘-kiâⁿ-ê ta̍k-ji̍t kiâⁿ \(66\) lí: Ke saⁿ-ji̍t sûi-lâng ū kiâⁿ kúi-lí? Koh saⁿ-lī kúi-lí? Tek \(285\), \(198\), \(87\).
  16. Ū lâng thàn chîⁿ \(561\), nā-sī hit-ji̍t só͘-hùi chiū-sī tia̍h-bí \(200\), bóe-bah \(100\), bóe-chhài \(30\), bóe-chhâ \(20\), bóe-tāu-koaⁿ \(15\), bóe-tāu-iû \(5\), taⁿ-chúi \(12\), tah-hé-iû \(24\): Iáu chhun jōa-chōe? Tek \(155\).
